About Dr. Anastasya Marinda

Dr. Anastasya Marinda is a highly experienced Endocrinologist with over 7 years of experience in the field. She specializes in the treatment and management of diseases and issues related to the human liver. Her role is crucial in maintaining the liver health of patients and providing the necessary care. She is currently the Head of the Endocrinology Department at Tokyo Hospital.
